# **App Name**: Cluck Campus

## Core Features:

- Order Customization: Allow users to select between whole chicken or a specific number of pieces. Price dynamically adjusts based on the selection.
- Reservation Slot: Implement a system for students to reserve a pick-up time slot for their chicken.
- AI - Recipe Suggestion: AI Tool suggest recipes using their chicken, and considering items they have listed in their order. Suggest different preparations and complementary side dishes. 
- Contact Form: Collect student's details like name, school hostel, block, room number, and phone number upon reserving their slot.
- Order History: Allows users to view their order history.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy Orange (#C45717), for the roasted color of the chicken and the warmth of the farm.
- Background color: Warm beige (#F2E8CF), desaturated for a natural feel.
- Accent color: Mustard Yellow (#D4AC0D) to accent details like buttons and other CTAs, complementing the orange to inspire a feeling of rustic culinary joy.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', serif for an elegant, intellectual, contemporary feel.
- Use icons to represent the type of chicken (whole/pieces) as well as options like spice levels and potential sides or meals that can be preordered
- Ensure a clear order flow, visually highlight order options
- Subtle animations to enhance the ordering experience.
